Overview

Sync App is a free desktop utility that helps you copy and synchronize folders on macOS using rsync. It is provided “as is” without warranties of any kind.

Use at Your Own Risk

  • You are responsible for choosing the correct source and destination.
  • Always verify your backups before deleting original data.
  • The author is not liable for data loss or damage.

Privacy

The app does not collect telemetry. All logs are stored locally on your device.
